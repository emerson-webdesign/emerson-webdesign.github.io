Red Roses

A fun poem

Roses are red violets are blue i like to walk and i like to run too

The pond is blue and so are you

The grass is green and i am mean

i sit in class like its mass waiting for the time to pass

i eat my lunch i eat like munch

I dont like dogs i like to cut logs

When the sky is blue i am too

You walk a path full of fame and they will know your name

poems i dont write so pick an apple and take a bite

this poem is done now i'll go for a run



By EMERSON
